Speical Edition: 2013 Spring Vacation - Disney World

Last Updated: Wednesday, May 8, 2013

This is a a speical edition of photos for you. We went to Disney World with Bekah's Parents (Joe & Beth) and Beth's Parents (Brad & Betty Ann). Thats 4 generations!. It was lots of fun. To make things even more exciting we took the Auto Train from northern Virginia to just north of Orlando. This is a non stop train that you can load your car onto and it was one of the main highlights for my train obsessed son. Morgan's favorite things were all train related, any train ride and the train at Magic Kingdom. He had a blast, and I am really proud of how well behaved he was. Shane did as well as you could hope, he slept in the stroller and didn't get too overtired.

Of special note are the night photos on Day 4 at Epcot. These were taken with a lens that my Grandpa Gregg got just before I was born.
